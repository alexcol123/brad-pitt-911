Brangelina: Brad Pitt & Angelina Jolie

The Rise and Fall of Hollywood's Most Famous Power Couple

The Beginning: Mr. & Mrs. Smith (2005)

Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie first met on the set of "Mr. & Mrs. Smith" in early 2004, where they played married spies who discover they've been assigned to kill each other. At the time, Pitt was married to Jennifer Aniston, and Jolie had recently ended her marriage to Billy Bob Thornton.

The chemistry between Pitt and Jolie was undeniable both on and off screen. While both stars initially denied any romantic involvement during filming, their relationship became public after Pitt's separation from Aniston was announced in January 2005. The timing of their relationship sparked intense media speculation and controversy.

Early Relationship & Public Debut (2005-2006)

In April 2005, paparazzi photos surfaced of Pitt, Jolie, and her son Maddox vacationing together in Kenya, effectively confirming their relationship. The couple became instant tabloid fodder, with the media dubbing them "Brangelina."

Their first major public appearance as a couple came in January 2006 when they were photographed together in Ethiopia, where Jolie was filming "Beyond Borders." The couple's relationship was characterized from the beginning by their shared commitment to humanitarian causes and international adoption.

Building a Family Together

The Adoptions

One of the defining aspects of Brangelina was their commitment to building a diverse, international family:

  • Maddox Chivan (born 2001) - Originally adopted by Jolie from Cambodia in 2002, Pitt legally adopted him in 2006
  • Zahara Marley (born 2005) - Adopted from Ethiopia in July 2005, with Pitt co-adopting in 2006
  • Pax Thien (born 2003) - Adopted from Vietnam in March 2007

Biological Children

The couple also had three biological children together:

  • Shiloh Nouvel (born May 27, 2006) - Their first biological child, born in Swakopmund, Namibia
  • Knox Léon and Vivienne Marcheline (born July 12, 2008) - Twins born in Nice, France

The birth of Shiloh made international headlines, with the first photos selling for $4.1 million to People magazine and Hello! magazine, with proceeds donated to charity. Similarly, the first photos of twins Knox and Vivienne sold for $14 million, the most expensive celebrity baby photos ever taken.

The Golden Years (2006-2012)

During their peak years as a couple, Brangelina became one of the most photographed and influential couples in the world. They were known for:

  • Red Carpet Power - Their appearances at premieres and award shows were major media events
  • Humanitarian Work - Joint efforts in disaster relief, refugee advocacy, and international aid
  • Real Estate Empire - Properties around the world including Château Miraval in France
  • Professional Collaborations - Jolie directed Pitt in "By the Sea" (2015)

The couple established the Jolie-Pitt Foundation, which made significant donations to humanitarian causes worldwide. They were particularly active in refugee advocacy, with Jolie serving as a UN Goodwill Ambassador and later Special Envoy.

The Wedding: Château Miraval (August 23, 2014)

After nearly nine years together and six children, Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie finally married on August 23, 2014, at their estate Château Miraval in Correns, France. The intimate ceremony was attended by their children and just 20 close friends and family members.

The couple had previously stated they wouldn't marry until marriage equality was legal everywhere, but eventually decided to wed for their children's sake. Jolie wore a custom Versace gown decorated with drawings made by their children, symbolizing their family's central role in their relationship.

Their wedding was kept secret from the media and only confirmed days later, marking a rare moment of privacy for the highly public couple.

Professional Collaborations

By the Sea (2015)

The couple's final professional collaboration came with "By the Sea," a drama written and directed by Angelina Jolie starring both actors. The film, about a troubled marriage, was shot in Malta and marked their first on-screen appearance together since "Mr. & Mrs. Smith."

Ironically, the film's themes of marital discord would prove prophetic, as the couple announced their separation just a year after its release.

Through Plan B Entertainment

While not directly collaborating, Jolie was involved in some Plan B Entertainment projects during their relationship, including humanitarian documentaries and socially conscious films that aligned with both of their values.

The Decline and Separation (2016)

Signs of trouble in the Brangelina marriage began to surface in 2015-2016. The couple appeared less frequently together at public events, and there were reports of tension during the filming of "By the Sea."

On September 15, 2016, an incident occurred on a private jet that would become central to their divorce proceedings. According to reports, an altercation took place between Pitt and Maddox, with allegations that Pitt was intoxicated and became physical with their eldest son.

Just days later, on September 19, 2016, Angelina Jolie filed for divorce from Brad Pitt, citing irreconcilable differences and requesting sole physical custody of their six children. The filing sent shockwaves through Hollywood and marked the end of one of the entertainment industry's most powerful couples.

The Divorce Battle (2016-2024)

Initial Proceedings and Investigations

Following Jolie's divorce filing, the FBI and Los Angeles County Department of Children and Family Services investigated the plane incident. Both agencies ultimately cleared Pitt of any wrongdoing, but the incident remained a source of contention throughout the divorce proceedings.

Custody Disputes

The most contentious aspect of their divorce involved custody of their children. Key developments included:

  • 2016-2018 - Initial custody arrangements with supervised visits for Pitt
  • 2018-2021 - Gradual increase in Pitt's custody time and unsupervised visits
  • 2021 - Temporary custody ruling granting Pitt more time with younger children
  • Maddox's choice - As the eldest child reached 18, he testified and chose limited contact with Pitt

Property and Financial Disputes

The couple's extensive assets became another source of conflict:

  • Château Miraval - Ongoing legal battle over the French vineyard and estate
  • Art Collection - Disputes over valuable artwork and furniture
  • Real Estate Portfolio - Division of multiple international properties
  • Business Interests - Separation of entertainment and production company assets

Public Statements and Media Coverage

Throughout the divorce proceedings, both parties generally maintained relative public silence, with occasional statements through representatives:

Brad Pitt's Position

  • Acknowledged personal struggles with alcohol and sought treatment
  • Expressed desire for privacy and focus on the children's wellbeing
  • Denied allegations of abuse while acknowledging the need for personal growth

Angelina Jolie's Position

  • Focused on protecting the children's safety and wellbeing
  • Maintained that her decisions were in the best interest of the family
  • Continued humanitarian work while dealing with personal challenges

Final Resolution (2024)

After eight years of legal battles, Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie's divorce was finally finalized in 2024. The resolution included:

  • Custody arrangements - Continued co-parenting with structured visitation
  • Financial settlement - Division of assets and ongoing support arrangements
  • Privacy agreements - Commitments to protect the children from media exposure

However, some disputes, particularly regarding Château Miraval, continue to work through the legal system as separate business matters.

Impact on Their Children

The Brangelina divorce had significant impacts on their six children:

  • Maddox - Now an adult, maintains limited relationship with Pitt
  • Pax - Has had a strained relationship with his adoptive father
  • Zahara - Continues to navigate the complex family dynamics
  • Shiloh - Has expressed desire for privacy and normal teenage life
  • Knox and Vivienne - The twins maintain relationships with both parents

Both parents have emphasized their commitment to co-parenting and protecting their children's privacy and wellbeing despite their personal differences.

Legacy of Brangelina

Cultural Impact

The Brangelina phenomenon represented more than just a celebrity relationship:

  • Humanitarian Influence - Raised global awareness for refugee issues and international aid
  • Adoption Advocacy - Inspired discussions about international adoption and diverse families
  • Media Evolution - Helped define modern celebrity culture and paparazzi coverage
  • Privacy vs. Fame - Highlighted the challenges of raising children in the public eye

Professional Legacy

  • Mr. & Mrs. Smith - Remains a popular action-comedy that launched their relationship
  • By the Sea - Their final collaboration, now viewed with added poignancy
  • Individual Success - Both continued successful careers during and after their relationship

Moving Forward (2024-Present)

With their divorce finalized, both Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie have focused on moving forward:

Brad Pitt

  • Continued successful acting career with films like "F1" (2025)
  • Expanded Plan B Entertainment with new partnerships
  • Focused on sobriety and personal growth
  • Maintained efforts to rebuild relationships with his children

Angelina Jolie

  • Continued directing and acting career
  • Ongoing humanitarian work as UN Special Envoy
  • Focus on raising their six children
  • Various business and philanthropic endeavors

Lessons and Reflections

The Brangelina story serves as both a fairy tale romance and a cautionary tale about fame, family, and the pressures of public life. Their 12-year relationship (2005-2016) was marked by genuine humanitarian efforts, a beautiful family, and significant cultural impact, but ultimately could not withstand the pressures of their celebrity status and personal differences.

Their story continues to evolve as they co-parent their children and pursue separate lives, serving as a reminder that even Hollywood's most glamorous relationships face real-world challenges that money and fame cannot solve.
