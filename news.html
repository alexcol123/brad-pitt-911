Latest Updates on Brad Pitt's Career and Life

2024-2025 Major Developments

Brad Pitt continues to make headlines with major film releases, business deals, and personal milestones. Here are the most significant recent developments in his career and life.

F1 (2025) - Box Office Success

Brad Pitt's Formula One racing drama "F1," directed by Joseph Kosinski, has become his highest-grossing film to date, earning nearly $600 million worldwide and surpassing "World War Z" ($540 million). The film features Pitt as an aging Formula One race car driver who returns to competition.

Pitt reportedly earned $30 million for the film, which was shot in collaboration with actual Formula One races for authentic racing sequences. The film's success has solidified Pitt's continued box office appeal well into his 60s.

Wolfs (2024) - Reunion with George Clooney

In September 2024, Brad Pitt reunited with longtime friend George Clooney for "Wolfs," an action-comedy thriller written and directed by Jon Watts. The film features the duo as rival fixers who are forced to work together on the same job.

The reunion marked another successful collaboration between the two stars, who have maintained a close friendship since their "Ocean's" trilogy days. Their on-screen chemistry and comedic timing were praised by critics and audiences alike.

Divorce Finalized (2024)

After an eight-year legal battle, Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie's divorce was finally finalized in 2024. The lengthy proceedings involved complex custody arrangements for their six children and the division of assets, including their French vineyard Château Miraval.

The conclusion of this high-profile divorce allows Pitt to move forward with his personal life while continuing to co-parent their children. The resolution removes a significant source of media attention that had followed him for nearly a decade.

Plan B Entertainment Sale to Mediawan

In a major business development, Brad Pitt's production company Plan B Entertainment was sold to French media conglomerate Mediawan in 2024. The deal, reportedly valued in the low hundreds of millions of dollars, sees Mediawan acquire a majority stake while Pitt retains a minority ownership.

This strategic partnership provides Plan B with additional resources for expansion while maintaining Pitt's creative involvement. The company continues to be led by co-presidents Dede Gardner and Jeremy Kleiner alongside Pitt.

Upcoming Projects

The Adventures of Cliff Booth

Pitt is set to return as Cliff Booth, his Oscar-winning character from Quentin Tarantino's "Once Upon a Time in Hollywood," in this Netflix crime comedy. The project will explore more adventures of the loyal stuntman character.

The Rider

An A24-backed film directed by Edward Berger, based on Tim Winton's novel. Pitt will play Fred Scully, who, after traveling across Europe for two years, lands in Ireland with his wife and daughter. The film promises to be a character-driven drama showcasing Pitt's dramatic acting abilities.

Various Plan B Projects

Through Plan B Entertainment, Pitt continues to develop numerous projects including "17 Sundays," "Sweet Heat," and season continuations of successful series like "Yellowjackets" and "The Creep Tapes."

Business Ventures & Investments

Château Miraval Vineyard

Pitt continues to co-own and operate the prestigious Château Miraval vineyard in Provence, France. The estate produces award-winning rosé wines that have gained international recognition. Despite his divorce from Angelina Jolie, Pitt maintains his involvement in the vineyard operations.

Real Estate Portfolio

Pitt maintains an extensive real estate portfolio, including his primary residence in Los Feliz, California, and properties in New Orleans. His interest in sustainable architecture continues to influence his property investments and development projects.

Industry Recognition & Status

With an estimated net worth of $400 million as of 2024, Brad Pitt remains one of Hollywood's most influential figures. His films have grossed over $9.3 billion worldwide, and he continues to command significant salaries for his acting roles while building Plan B Entertainment into a major force in independent filmmaking.

At 61, Pitt shows no signs of slowing down, with multiple high-profile projects in development and a continued commitment to meaningful storytelling through his production company.

Personal Life Updates

Following the conclusion of his divorce proceedings, Pitt has maintained a relatively private personal life while focusing on his relationship with his children and his various professional and humanitarian commitments. He continues to prioritize his sobriety and personal wellness while remaining one of Hollywood's most sought-after talents.

Looking Ahead

As Brad Pitt enters 2025, he remains at the top of his game with a successful film career, thriving production company, and continued commitment to meaningful causes. His recent box office success with "F1" proves his enduring appeal, while his upcoming projects promise to showcase different aspects of his talents as both actor and producer.

With Plan B Entertainment's expansion under its new partnership with Mediawan and exciting projects in development, Pitt's influence in Hollywood continues to grow beyond his acting career into producing, environmental advocacy, and social justice initiatives.
