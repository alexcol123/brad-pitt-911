Plan B Entertainment

Award-Winning Production Company Behind Groundbreaking Cinema

Company Overview

Plan B Entertainment is an American production company founded in November 2001 by Brad Pitt, Brad Grey, Kristin Hahn, and Jennifer Aniston. After Pitt and Aniston's divorce in 2005 and Grey's departure to become CEO of Paramount Pictures, Brad Pitt became the sole owner of the company.

The company is currently led by Dede Gardner and Jeremy Kleiner as co-presidents, alongside Pitt. Plan B has established itself as one of Hollywood's most successful independent production companies, known for producing socially conscious, critically acclaimed films and television series.

Recent Business Development (2024)

In a major business development, Plan B Entertainment was sold to French media conglomerate Mediawan in 2024. Mediawan acquired a majority stake in Plan B in a deal reportedly valued in the low hundreds of millions of dollars. Brad Pitt retained a minority stake in the company, ensuring his continued involvement while providing resources for expansion.

Company Philosophy

Plan B Entertainment has built its reputation on producing films that tackle important social issues while maintaining high artistic standards. The company consistently focuses on:

  • Social Justice - Stories that address inequality, civil rights, and human dignity
  • Diverse Voices - Supporting directors and writers from underrepresented communities
  • Quality Storytelling - Prioritizing narrative excellence and artistic integrity
  • Cultural Impact - Creating films that spark conversation and promote understanding

Awards and Recognition

Plan B Entertainment's commitment to excellence has earned numerous accolades:

  • 3 Academy Awards for Best Picture - The Departed, 12 Years a Slave, Moonlight
  • Multiple BAFTA Awards - Including Best Film honors
  • Golden Globe Awards - Best Picture wins and nominations
  • Primetime Emmy Awards - For television productions
  • Independent Spirit Awards - Recognition for independent filmmaking excellence

Legacy and Impact

Since its founding over two decades ago, Plan B Entertainment has established itself as a powerhouse in independent film production. The company's films have not only achieved critical acclaim and commercial success but have also contributed to important cultural conversations about race, sexuality, economic inequality, and human rights.

Under the leadership of Brad Pitt, Dede Gardner, and Jeremy Kleiner, Plan B continues to be one of Hollywood's most respected production companies, with a track record that demonstrates both artistic excellence and commercial viability.
