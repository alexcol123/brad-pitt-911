Legacy & Impact

Brad Pitt's awards and recognition span over three decades, showcasing his evolution from a breakthrough supporting actor to one of Hollywood's most respected leading men and producers. His accolades reflect not only his talent as a performer but also his commitment to producing meaningful, impactful cinema through Plan B Entertainment.

With films grossing over $9.3 billion worldwide and a collection of prestigious awards, Pitt has established himself as one of the most successful and influential figures in modern cinema.
