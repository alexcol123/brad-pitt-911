Filmography

30+ Years of Iconic Performances

Career Highlights (1991-2025)

Brad Pitt's filmography spans over three decades, featuring diverse roles from romantic leads to complex character studies. His films have grossed over $9.3 billion worldwide, making him one of the most bankable stars in Hollywood history.

Thelma & Louise

1991

Breakthrough role as J.D., the charming cowboy hitchhiker. Directed by Ridley Scott, this film launched Pitt into stardom.

A River Runs Through It

1992

Dramatic role in Robert Redford's adaptation of Norman Maclean's novella about fly fishing and family relationships.

Legends of the Fall

1994

Epic drama as Tristan Ludlow, showcasing his leading man potential in this sweeping family saga.

Interview with the Vampire

1994

Gothic horror film alongside Tom Cruise, demonstrating his versatility in the supernatural genre.

Seven

1995

Psychological thriller with Morgan Freeman. His role as Detective David Mills in this dark crime drama was critically acclaimed.

12 Monkeys

1995

Terry Gilliam's sci-fi thriller earned Pitt a Golden Globe for Best Supporting Actor and an Oscar nomination.

Fight Club

1999

Iconic role as Tyler Durden in David Fincher's cult classic about masculinity and consumer culture.

Snatch

2000

Guy Ritchie's crime comedy featuring Pitt as Mickey, an Irish bare-knuckle boxer with an incomprehensible accent.

Ocean's Eleven

2001

Steven Soderbergh's heist film alongside George Clooney. The chemistry between the leads spawned two sequels.

Troy

2004

Epic historical drama as Achilles. Despite mixed reviews, it was a major box office success globally.

Mr. & Mrs. Smith

2005

Action-comedy with Angelina Jolie about married spies. The film led to their real-life relationship.

Babel

2006

Alejandro González Iñárritu's interconnected drama earned Pitt an Oscar nomination for Best Supporting Actor.

The Curious Case of Benjamin Button

2008

David Fincher's fantasy drama about a man aging backwards. Earned Pitt an Oscar nomination for Best Actor.

Inglourious Basterds

2009

Quentin Tarantino's World War II film featuring Pitt as Lieutenant Aldo Raine, leader of the Basterds.

Moneyball

2011

Sports drama as Oakland A's manager Billy Beane. Earned him another Oscar nomination for Best Actor.

World War Z

2013

Zombie apocalypse thriller that became Pitt's highest-grossing film until F1, earning $540 million worldwide.

Fury

2014

World War II tank crew drama directed by David Ayer, showcasing Pitt's intensity in a gritty war setting.

The Big Short

2015

Financial crisis drama about the 2008 housing bubble. The ensemble cast earned a SAG Award nomination.

Allied

2016

World War II romantic thriller with Marion Cotillard, directed by Robert Zemeckis.

Once Upon a Time in Hollywood

2019

Tarantino's ode to 1960s Hollywood. Pitt won the Oscar for Best Supporting Actor as stuntman Cliff Booth.

Ad Astra

2019

Sci-fi psychological drama about an astronaut's journey to find his father. A contemplative space epic.

Bullet Train

2022

Action-comedy thriller set on a Japanese bullet train, featuring Pitt as an unlucky assassin named Ladybug.

Babylon

2022

Damien Chazelle's epic about Hollywood's transition from silent films to talkies, reuniting Pitt with Margot Robbie.

Wolfs

2024

Action-comedy reuniting Pitt with George Clooney as rival fixers forced to work together.

F1

2025

Formula One racing drama directed by Joseph Kosinski. Pitt plays an aging driver returning to competition. Grossed nearly $600 million worldwide.

Box Office Impact

Brad Pitt's films have been consistently successful at the global box office. His top-grossing films include F1 (2025) with nearly $600 million, World War Z with $540 million, and Troy with $497 million worldwide. His ability to draw audiences across different genres has made him one of Hollywood's most reliable stars.
