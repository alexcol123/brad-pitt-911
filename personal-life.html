Personal Life

Family, Relationships, and Life Beyond the Camera

High-Profile Relationships

Brad Pitt's personal life has been subject to intense media scrutiny, particularly his marriages to two of Hollywood's biggest stars and his role as a father to six children.

Marriage to Jennifer Aniston (2000-2005)

Brad Pitt married actress Jennifer Aniston on July 29, 2000, in a lavish ceremony in Malibu, California. The couple was considered one of Hollywood's golden pairs and were frequently in the spotlight during their marriage.

During their marriage, they co-founded Plan B Entertainment in 2001, initially as a joint venture with producer Brad Grey. The couple announced their separation in January 2005, and their divorce was finalized in October 2005. Their split was highly publicized, especially in relation to Pitt's subsequent relationship with Angelina Jolie.

Relationship and Marriage to Angelina Jolie (2005-2016)

Brad Pitt began a relationship with actress Angelina Jolie after they met on the set of "Mr. & Mrs. Smith" in 2005. The couple, dubbed "Brangelina" by the media, became one of the most photographed and talked-about couples in the world.

Together, they raised six children - three adopted (Maddox, Pax, and Zahara) and three biological (Shiloh, and twins Knox and Vivienne). The couple married in August 2014 at their French estate, Château Miraval, in a private ceremony.

In September 2016, Jolie filed for divorce, citing irreconcilable differences. The divorce proceedings were lengthy and often contentious, involving custody disputes over their children. After an eight-year legal battle, their divorce was finally finalized in 2024.

Children

Brad Pitt is the father of six children, three adopted and three biological:

  • Maddox Chivan (born 2001) - Adopted from Cambodia in 2002, originally by Jolie, then by Pitt after their relationship began
  • Pax Thien (born 2003) - Adopted from Vietnam in 2007
  • Zahara Marley (born 2005) - Adopted from Ethiopia in 2005
  • Shiloh Nouvel (born 2006) - First biological child with Angelina Jolie
  • Knox Léon and Vivienne Marcheline (born 2008) - Twins, their second and third biological children

Pitt has spoken openly about the joys and challenges of fatherhood, and his children have been a central part of his life. The custody arrangements following his divorce from Jolie have been a significant aspect of the legal proceedings.

Properties and Lifestyle

Brad Pitt has owned several notable properties throughout his career:

  • Château Miraval, France - A vineyard estate in Provence where he married Angelina Jolie
  • Los Feliz Compound, California - His primary residence, a sprawling estate he's owned since the 1990s
  • New Orleans Properties - Various properties in the French Quarter and surrounding areas

Known for his interest in architecture and design, Pitt has been involved in various real estate and development projects, including humanitarian housing efforts in New Orleans after Hurricane Katrina.

Humanitarian Work

Beyond his entertainment career, Brad Pitt has been actively involved in various humanitarian causes:

  • Make It Right Foundation - Founded to build affordable, environmentally friendly housing in New Orleans' Lower 9th Ward after Hurricane Katrina
  • The Jolie-Pitt Foundation - Established with Angelina Jolie to support humanitarian causes worldwide
  • Global poverty and disaster relief - Significant donations to various international relief efforts
  • LGBTQ+ rights advocacy - Support for marriage equality and LGBTQ+ organizations
  • Environmental causes - Support for sustainable living and environmental protection

Personal Interests

Brad Pitt has cultivated various personal interests outside of acting:

  • Architecture and Design - Passionate about sustainable architecture and has studied furniture design
  • Motorcycles - Avid motorcycle enthusiast and collector
  • Wine Making - Co-owner of Château Miraval vineyard, producing award-winning rosé wines
  • Art Collection - Collector of contemporary art and furniture
  • Aviation - Licensed pilot who enjoys flying

Health and Wellness

Pitt has been open about his personal struggles and growth:

  • Sobriety - Has spoken about giving up alcohol and attending Alcoholics Anonymous meetings
  • Therapy - Has discussed the benefits of therapy and personal growth work
  • Fitness - Maintains an active lifestyle and is known for his dedication to physical fitness for film roles

Current Status

As of 2024-2025, Brad Pitt continues to focus on his career, his children, and his various business and humanitarian interests. With an estimated net worth of $400 million, he remains one of Hollywood's most influential figures while maintaining a relatively private personal life following the conclusion of his high-profile divorce proceedings.

He continues to be selective about his relationships and maintains his commitment to co-parenting his children while pursuing meaningful projects both in entertainment and philanthropy.
